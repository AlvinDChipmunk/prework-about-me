Greetings Indie Movie Fans!

Cinemason Productions is a non union group of filmmakers located in San Diego. We specialize in creating original film content for film festivals, 48 hour projects, YouTube, and beyond. We here at Cinemason, strive to reach higher ground with our skills and show the world new captivating masterpieces of art. Welcome to Cinemason!

Our Founder

Michael "Mace" Mason

Michael Mason has been studying film for several years. He started out with a camera and a dream to make a feature film. Thus the film "Whitefire" was born. In that process, he has learned editing, cinematography, directing, writing, acting, visual effects, and many more talents.

His editing has been recognized by Lionsgate, and his work has been showcased several times at the San Diego Comic Con. With all this talent, and many projects later, Cinemason productions was created.
