Renaissance Faire
My gaming group decided a long time ago to first try live action role playing, but then found that they liked doing Ren Faires with stage safe metal weapons and teaching and demonstrating Medieval and Renaissance history. So, they created a fictional Swiss mercenary group called Rosenkeep. We have been participating for several San Diego based Faires, with the latest being the Escondido semiannual Faire, now for the past ten years. With that interest, we each had to deal with the expenses of props and costuming, which now leads to another hobby, which is...
Leather Working and Armoring
While figuring out what kinds of equipment would be needed to have a good Renaissance Faire camp and costume, that led to learning how to shape, cut, and dye leather items, including armor. And, while studying different armors including leather, I got to learn different techniques that led to shaping metal as well, to make plate and chain mail items.
